Who We Are

Trees & Bees is a small, family-owned producer of natural honey and maple syrup products based in Bonduel, Wisconsin. For us, making maple syrup and honey is more than just a business - it's a family tradition that has been passed down for generations.

Learn more about our story here

What We Do

Our products are collected and produced in small batches to ensure quality and freshness. All our products are produced and packaged with sustainability in mind. Our goal, beyond making delicious honey and maple syrup, is to protect our environment and ecosystems while educating others about the importance of sustainability and supporting sustainable businesses.
