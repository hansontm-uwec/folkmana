Our Process

Our maple products all start in the same place - our maple trees. Each spring, our 10 acres of maple trees produce nearly 500 gallons of maple syrup! We make our maple products using a tried and tested family recipe and use a small batch process to check and test our products at every step of the production process to ensure quality and great flavor. All our maple products are certified 100% organic and contain no added sugar or preservatives. In other words, you know exactly what's in the jar.
