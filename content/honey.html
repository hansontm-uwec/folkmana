Our Process

Our honey & beeswax products are the culmination of a hard day's work of our good bee friends. Our operation currently owns about 120 hives full of honey bees who produce 600 gallons of honey and about 60 pounds of beeswax per season! We make all of our honey products using a tried and tested family recipe and use a small batch process to check and test our products at every step of the production process to ensure quality and great flavor. All our honey products are certified 100% organic and our honey contains no added sugar or preservatives. In other words, you know exactly what's in the jar.
